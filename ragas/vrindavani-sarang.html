Notation guide: S R G M P D N are shuddha swars. Lowercase r, g, d, n indicate komal swars. M^ indicates tivra Madhyam. S' indicates upper Sa.

Explanation

How to understand Raga Vrindavani Sarang

Raga Vrindavani Sarang is bright and devotional, with a smooth and pastoral character. The absence of G and D gives it openness, while the two forms of Nishad add a memorable color.

Features of the Raga

  1. Raga Vrindavani Sarang omits G and D, creating a distinctive pentatonic sound.
  2. The use of N in ascent and n in descent gives the raga a graceful color.
  3. The phrase N S' n P is an important identity phrase.
  4. It has a devotional and pastoral feeling, often associated with afternoon.
