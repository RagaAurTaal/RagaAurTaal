How to Use This Section

Start with mood, then study the structure.

Each raga page includes the traditional learning fields: Thaat, Vikrut Swar, Varjit Swar, Jati, Vadi, Samvadi, Time, Aaroha, Avaroha, Pakad, and Features of the Raga.

Notation can vary slightly by teacher, gharana, and syllabus. These pages are meant to be a clear study guide and listening introduction.
